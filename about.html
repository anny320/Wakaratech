Who We Are

About Wakara

Built on 12+ years at the intersection of learning & technology.

Built on 12+ years at the intersection of learning & technology

Wakara Technologies is a Learning Engineering as a Service (LEaaS) company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We partner with EdTech leaders, public institutions, and innovation teams to design, build, and scale learning products that work.

Our work spans curriculum design, product engineering, digital transformation, and accessibility — bringing together the disciplines that modern learning organizations need under one roof.

We believe that great learning is engineered, not just created. That means treating learning products with the same rigour, iteration, and evidence-based thinking that world-class technology teams apply to software. It's this philosophy — Learning Engineering — that drives everything we do.
