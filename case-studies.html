Eneza Education

Scaled SMS-based learning to 1.3M+ learners across Kenya and West Africa. Built operations, partnerships, and growth systems from Series A through Series B — creating one of the continent's most widely adopted mobile learning platforms.

As COO, led the expansion of the product from a Kenyan pilot to a multi-country business, including government partnerships, telco integrations, and a content library spanning primary and secondary curriculum across multiple languages.

CANEX / IATF

Designed and delivered digital learning experiences for African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) business communities through the CANEX platform — reaching traders, entrepreneurs, and policymakers across 54 African nations.

The engagement spanned learning experience design, content architecture, and digital delivery strategy for audiences with highly varied connectivity and digital literacy levels. A flagship use case for accessible, Africa-first learning design.
