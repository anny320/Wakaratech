Questions

Common questions

How quickly do you respond?

Within 24 hours on business days. If you send a message on a Friday, expect to hear from us Monday morning Nairobi time (EAT).

Do you work with organisations outside Kenya?

Yes — we're remote-first. Our work spans Africa and beyond. If your learning challenge is the right fit, geography isn't a constraint.

What's the minimum engagement size?

Our Discovery package (2-week sprint) is the entry point for new partnerships. For very small organisations, we occasionally take on pro-bono advisory work — ask on the call.

Do you only work in EdTech?

Our expertise is deepest in EdTech, but we work with any organisation that has a significant learning challenge — corporate L&D, public sector training, health worker education, and more.

Learning Engineering as a Service. We partner with EdTech leaders, public institutions, and innovation teams to design, build, and scale learning products that work.
