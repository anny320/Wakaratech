What We Offer

Services

Full-spectrum LEaaS coverage — from strategy through scale.

Service Areas

Five disciplines, one integrated practice

Each service area is deep in its own right — and most powerful when they work together.

💡

Learning Experience Design

Human-centered design of learning journeys, curriculum frameworks, and content architectures that drive real outcomes. We apply instructional design theory, learning science, and UX research to create experiences that learners actually engage with — not just content they scroll past. From needs analysis and learner personas through to storyboarding, prototype testing, and final content production.

⚡

Digital Transformation Strategy

Roadmaps and change management for organizations moving from traditional to digital-first learning ecosystems. Whether you're a national curriculum authority digitizing textbooks, a corporate L&D team replacing classroom training, or an NGO scaling a field program — we help you define the destination, build stakeholder alignment, and sequence the work. Strategy without execution is just a document; we stay through implementation.

🧪

Product & Innovation Engineering

End-to-end product development — from prototype to production — built on agile, user-research-driven methodologies. We run discovery sprints, define MVPs, build working prototypes, conduct usability testing, and shepherd products through to launch and iteration. Our team brings together product management, engineering oversight, UX design, and learning design into a single integrated squad.

👁

Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, low-bandwidth optimization, and inclusive UX patterns for learners in every context. We audit existing products for accessibility gaps, design remediation roadmaps, and build accessibility into new products from day one. This means screen-reader compatibility, keyboard navigation, color contrast, offline-first architecture, SMS fallback, and support for learners with varying digital literacy levels.

📈

Growth & Scale Operations

Partnership strategy, market expansion playbooks, and operations systems that take learning products from pilot to pan-African scale. Drawing on direct experience scaling Eneza Education from seed to Series B, we help EdTech companies build the institutional relationships, data infrastructure, and team capabilities needed to grow. This includes government partnership strategy, telco integrations, and community-based distribution models.

Engagement Models

Choose your package

Structured engagements that fit where you are — from early discovery to long-term partnership.

Package 01

Discovery

A focused 2-week sprint to map your learning landscape, understand your learners, and define your LEaaS roadmap. Deliverables include a landscape audit, learner personas, and a prioritized opportunity framework. Perfect for organizations just getting started or re-evaluating their strategy.

Package 02

Accelerate

An 8-week engagement to design, prototype, and test your core learning experience. We move from insights to a validated prototype that you can take to investors, government partners, or learners. Ideal for teams that have a product vision and need to move fast with confidence.

Package 03

Transform

A 16-week full build — from strategy through to a launch-ready product. This is our most comprehensive single-engagement package, covering discovery, design, build, and launch. You end with a live product, a user-tested experience, and an operations playbook for what comes next.

Package 04

Enterprise

Ongoing LEaaS partnership — an embedded team, quarterly strategy sessions, and full delivery capability. For organizations that need a sustained learning engineering function without building one in-house. We act as your external LEaaS team, adapting to your priorities quarter by quarter.
