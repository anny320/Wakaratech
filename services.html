HCD Studio

Human-centred design research, facilitation, and co-creation — applied before, during, and after product development. We run discovery research, user interviews, co-design workshops, usability testing, and synthesis. The HCD Studio is where the insight work happens: understanding people, their contexts, and the gaps between what exists and what's actually needed.

Engagement Models

How we engage

Structured engagements that fit where you are — from early discovery to long-term partnership.

Package 01

A focused 2-week sprint to map your landscape, understand your users, and define a clear opportunity framework. Deliverables: landscape audit, user personas, prioritised roadmap. Right for teams getting started or re-evaluating direction.

Package 02

An 8-week engagement to design, prototype, and test your core product experience. You end with a validated prototype ready for investors, partners, or users — built with confidence, not guesswork.

Package 03

A 16-week full build — discovery through launch. Strategy, design, build, and an operations playbook for what comes next.

Package 04

An ongoing embedded partnership — quarterly strategy, full delivery capability, and a team that adapts to your priorities. For organisations that need a sustained product function without building one in-house.
